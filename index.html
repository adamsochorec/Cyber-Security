Avoiding beeing victim of the social engeneering


Watch out for your online traces.

Set your personal information (such as birthday, year of graduation, etc.) as private or at least not public (friends/followers) NO friends of friends (you do not control the information accessibility scope!).

Do not pick your pins or passwords as keywords you published somewhere.

When registering for a new online service, fill out only the required information, the less you say about yourself, the better.

When instaling a new app, checkout it's requests to read your data - decide which are necessary for the app functionality.
